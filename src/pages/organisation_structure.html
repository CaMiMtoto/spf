Organisation Structure

  • Obj 1 Building strong linkages among seed value chain actors

    (By the end of 2021, The Company will be able to make 200 contracts agreements between seed producers of different categories with SPF- IKIGEGA).

  • Obj 2 Ensure access to quality seeds

    (By the end of 2021, the company will have supported, 200 Individual Seed Multipliers, 100 famers groups, 50 cooperatives of seed multipliers to improve the quality of produced seeds in order to access to more profitable and reliable markets.

  • Obj 3 Ensure Market to Seed Multipliers

    (By the end of 2021, the company will have supported, organized and source from 200 entrepreneurs seed multipliers (among others the 30 screen houses owners); 60,000tons of basic seeds will be produced).

  • Obj 4 Ensure Potato seed Storage and handling

    (By end of 2021 the company will have the capacity of storing per year 60,000 Tons of potato seeds in good conditions).

  • Obj 5 Ensure sales and distribution of Potato seed

    ((By end of 2021 the company will be able to supply per year 60,000 Tons of quality potato seeds)

  • Obj 6 Strengthening the Company Management

    (The SPF Management team and Board members, will got constantly the required updated knowledge capacity to manage efficiently the company).
